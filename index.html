Our Story

Built for a smarter,

QuickPull was founded in 2022 by engineers and urban planners tired of seeing empty cars clog city streets. Our mission is simple: make ride-sharing so seamless and affordable that choosing to share becomes the obvious choice.

Today, QuickPull operates in 12 cities across India, facilitating over 2,000 rides every single day. Our community of verified drivers and riders grows by thousands each week.
